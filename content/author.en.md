## Page Purpose

This page is meant to introduce the author in a fuller and more personal way than the homepage.

## Suggested Structure

### 1. Short Introduction

Start with a compact introduction that answers:

- who you are
- what fields you focus on
- how you relate to PaintersGO

### 2. Background

You can expand on:

- academic or professional background
- how you got into 3D, video, AR, design or development
- why you continue exploring this creative direction

### 3. Personal Perspective

This section is a good place to share your view on creativity, technology, product thinking or experience design.

### 4. Your Role in PaintersGO

Useful topics include:

- what role you play in the project
- which parts matter most to you
- what kind of impact you hope the project will create

### 5. Contact Information

You can later add:

- email
- social accounts
- GitHub
- portfolio links
